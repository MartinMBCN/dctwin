How to work with your Digital Career Twin

Build your Twin

Begin by giving the Twin as much evidence as you already have.

This can include:

  • a CV, in PDF or DOCX format;
  • pasted career history;
  • additional CVs;
  • manually added achievements;
  • later, other professional evidence.

The Twin reconciles all of these into a single canonical representation of your professional history.

Develop your Twin

Once the initial Twin exists, continue improving it through guided interviews.

The interview is not a questionnaire. It is an ongoing conversation driven by the Twin itself.

You might choose to explore:

  • a particular role;
  • your past;
  • your current professional identity;
  • your future aspirations.

Or simply allow the Twin to determine the next most valuable question.

The objective is always the same: build the most accurate representation of your professional life possible.

Reflect on your Twin

At any time you can open the Mirror. The Mirror is the current reflection of your Twin.

It presents:

  • your Overview Brief;
  • career timeline;
  • evidence;
  • capabilities;
  • preferences;
  • objectives;
  • other reflections as they evolve.

Nothing in the Mirror is immutable. It is intended to be questioned, refined and negotiated until it becomes an increasingly faithful reflection of your professional identity.

Explore your Twin (future)

Once the Twin becomes sufficiently rich, additional ways of understanding it become possible.

Visualizations may include:

  • capability maps;
  • objective alignment;
  • progression over time;
  • strengths and gaps;
  • comparisons;
  • network views;
  • other visual representations.

These visualizations are not the Twin. They are alternative ways of understanding the same underlying model.
